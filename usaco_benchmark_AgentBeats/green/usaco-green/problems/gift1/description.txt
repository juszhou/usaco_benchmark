A group of NP people are exchanging gifts. Each person gives gifts to some others. The amount given is divided equally among the receivers, with the remainder kept by the giver.

Input:
- First line: NP (1 <= NP <= 10)
- Next NP lines: names of people
- Then, for each person: a line with giver name, then a line with NG (number of receivers), then NG lines each with receiver name and amount.

Output: For each person, their name and net gift amount (received - given).
